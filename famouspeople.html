The famous people from the village




There have been plenty of famous people that share origins in the village,

ranging from a folklorist, a horse race trainer, and a man who holds a claim as having the fourth highest iq on the planet.



Patrick Kennedy (1801-1873)


Patrick Kennedy was a folklorist, which meant he held collection of various folktales and lore.

While he wasn't exactly born in Clonroche, being orginally from Kilmyshall, he did grow up in the village and was schooled there too.

Robert published many collections of folktales and folklore in his years.

His works include; Legends of Mount Leinster(1855), Fictions of Our Forefathers(1859), Legendary fictions of the Irish Celts (1867), etc.




Aidan O'Brien (1969 -)


Aidan O'Brien is a horse race trainer, often hailed as one of the bests.


He has trained over 30 successful horses over the years.


He has been crowned as England's Champion Trainer, the last irishman to recieve that title was Vincent O'Brien (no relation) in 1971
