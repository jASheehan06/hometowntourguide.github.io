The Cloch Bán


Known locally as Judes or Ffrench's


This is one of two pubs in the village.


This was founded in the 1650s, making it one of the oldest known buildings in the village.

As usual to serve pints, it also has a restaurant you can order from.

A lounge opens up at the left side of the building for many to enter.


A pool table can be found in a corner area of the lounge.

The door on the right side of the building in the entrance to the main pub, holding a warm fireplace for if you're looking for a traditional pub experience.
