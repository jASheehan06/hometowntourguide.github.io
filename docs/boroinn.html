The Boro Inn


Also known locally as Paddy's


This pub was established in 1877 and has been run by 6 generations of the Doyle family since opening

The Boro Inn was named after the banks of the boro, which is also how Castleboro got its name from.

The pub has many activites to use such as:


A pool table with the occasional tournament,


A beer garden,


And open mics for bands to perform



There is also a lounge the left side of the building, only open for parties and events.


The right is the main pub.
